    The Wild Girls By Phoebe Morgan


    Four Friends. A Luxury Retreat. It's going to be murder

    Synopis From Back of Book.

    It's been years since Grace, Felicity, Alice, and Hanna were together.
    The "Wild Girls". As they were once called, are no longer so wild.
    Alice is a teacher. Hanna has a new baby. Grace is a homebody.
    Only Felicity, seemes to have retained her former spark.

    Then Felicity invites them all on the weekend of a lifetime.
    A birthday bash in Bostwana. It will be a chance to have fun and
    rekindle their once bomb proof friendship... and finally put that one
    horrible night, all those years ago, behind them for good.

    But soon after arriving at the luxury safari lodge, a feeling of unease
    settles over them. There's no sign of the party that was promised.
    There's no phone signal. They are on their own... and things start to
    go very, very wrong.

    If you are a fan of Lucy Foley. This book is for you!

    The Wanted


    Investigator Elivs Cole and his partner Joe Pike are back!
    But this could be the deadlest case of their career.

    Synopis From Back of Book

    When single mother Devon Connor hires Elvis Cole, it's because her
    troubled teenage son Tyson is flasing cash and she's afraid he's dealing
    drugs. But the truth is devastatingly different. With two others, he's been
    responsible for a string of high-end buglaries, a crime spree that takes
    a deadly turn when one of them is murdered and Tyson and his girlfriend
    disappear.

    They stole the wrong thing from the wrong man and determined to get it back,
    he has hired two men who are smart and brutal and the best and what they do.

    To even the odds, Cole brings in his friend Joe Pike, but even the two of them
    together may be overmatched. The police don't want them anywhere near the
    investigation. The teens refuse to be found, and the killers are leaving a trail
    of bodies in their wake. Pretty soon, they'll find out everything they need to
    track the kids down - and then nothing that Elvis or Joe can do may make
    and difference. It might even get them killed.
